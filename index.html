About me

I'm a user experience researcher and designer. I'm currently a PhD student in Human-Computer Interaction at Stanford University, where I work with James Landay and other amazing people in the HCI Group. My research focuses on understanding how people interact with AI systems, with the goal of designing more transparent, trustworthy, and equitable AI technologies. Before coming to Stanford, I worked as a UX designer and researcher at various tech companies and startups, including Google, Airbnb, and the MIT Media Lab. I'm also passionate about diversity and inclusion in tech, and have led several initiatives to promote equity and representation for underrepresented groups in the industry.
